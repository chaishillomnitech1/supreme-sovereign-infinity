# Anchor DAO Regulations

## Governance Rules

1. **Policy Codification**: All DAO policies must be formally documented within the governance rules to ensure transparency and accountability.
2. **Decision Making**: Proposals to amend policies should be put forward to all DAO members for discussion and voting.
3. **Voting Process**: Each member is entitled to one vote. Major policy changes require a 75% majority for approval.
4. **Compliance**: All members must adhere to the established governance rules, with non-compliance leading to potential sanctions.
5. **Amendments**: These governance rules may be amended through a majority vote, provided all members are notified at least one week in advance of the vote.
6. **Review Cycle**: These rules are subject to review every six months to ensure they remain relevant and effective.
