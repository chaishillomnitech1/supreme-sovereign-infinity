# AGENTS

This document acknowledges the ancestral guidance that shapes our responsibilities within this community.

## Responsibilities:

1. **Teaching:**  
   - To impart knowledge passed down through generations, ensuring it is preserved and honored.
2. **Wisdom:**  
   - To apply age-old insights in making informed decisions that benefit the entire community.
3. **Mentoring:**  
   - To guide others in understanding their roles and how they align with ancestral teachings.
4. **Support:**  
   - To provide assistance to those seeking knowledge and understanding, fostering an environment of learning.

By fulfilling these responsibilities, we honor those who came before us and reassert our commitment to their teachings.
